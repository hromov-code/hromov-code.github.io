What is SIMIA?

SIMIA is the audio and video calling system based on decentralized blockchain technology and direct p2p communication protocol. You can monetize your expertise and content, organize group calls or paid audio and video streams, build social networks, etc.

All of this without middlemen involved. Safe and transparent payment system based on a stable unified token SIMIA allows to process payments from any part of the globe, avoiding banking fees or jurisdictional payment limitations.

Payments received in SIMIA tokens can be converted into any standard (US dollar, euro, etc.) or crypto-currency with one click. Earned funds can be transferred from online SIMIA wallet to a banking account in any country. This system overcomes geographical barriers and creates a border-free space for online communication worldwide.

How does it work?

SIMIA network is based on a fully automated system of remote servers, called master nodes or Peer Connectors. It is a p2p decentralized network of hundreds of such nodes. These nodes are connected, independent from each other and owned by separate individuals. Node owners join the network and provide users with instruments for audio and video calls. They earn a commission of 1.13% from each communications session. Payments are processed using safe and transparent blockchain technology: from one online wallet to another. Interactions happen within decentralized applications (DApps) that display information about network participants.

Online application SIMIA Space provides a marketplace and an expert explorer that allows users to find experts and establish a paid connection with them. This interface is one example of a decentralized application (DApp) deployed onto the network. Many other DApps can be developed in a similar fashion to give rise to many more social and expert networks based on SIMIA protocol.

How do you communicate?

Step 1

Expert establishes tariff per minute or per session (example: 1 min = 1 SIMIA token)

Step 2

Upon agreement to communicate, SIMIA Peer Connector node connects the participant and an expert in an encrypted communication channel (video or audio stream).

Step 3

Expert receives an invitation to connect from another user (video or audio call) Note: A participant may request a contact only if SIMIA network confirms the availability of funds in the account for a minimum of 1 minute of communication

Step 4

If the connection is successful, Peer Connector freezes the funds of the participant(s) in personal or group calls every minute until the connection is disconnected or there is not enough balance for the next minute of the conversation with an expert

After the connection is completed, the network, through blockchain technology, distributes and credits funds to your address, deducting the Peer Connector node commission of 1.13% for processing and confirming transactions in a distributed network by contacting all SIMIA Peer Connector nodes. The network is fully distributed and does not have control nodes. There is no way to block your address on the SIMIA Network. The ability to ban the connection is missing. Network disconnection is possible only in case of disconnection of all network nodes (probability of this event is 0.099% with 1000 nodes).
